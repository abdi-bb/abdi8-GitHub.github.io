Key Features

Admin Dashboard for Car, Customer, and Booking Management

The Admin Dashboard is the command center of WheelsOnRent, providing administrators with comprehensive control over the entire car rental system. Easily oversee the vehicle fleet, ensure their availability, and manage bookings efficiently, all from one centralized hub.

Customer Access to Car Listings

Empowering customers with choice, our platform offers an extensive catalog of vehicles. Each listing includes detailed information, images, and specifications, ensuring customers can make informed decisions when selecting the perfect car for their needs.

Effortless Booking for Customers

Booking a car with WheelsOnRent is a breeze. Our platform simplifies the reservation process, allowing customers to secure their preferred vehicle with just a few clicks. Select your pickup and drop-off dates, review pricing and availability, and finalize your booking hassle-free.
